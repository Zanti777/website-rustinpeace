Community Roles

Join our community and collect Editions.

In the Rust In Peace community, the number of NFTs you hold determines your role within the discord group. Verified holders of Rust In Peace NFTs have access to the RIP Holders Channel, where they can participate in discussions and connect with other verified holders. Additionally, verified holders will have the ability to vote, giving them a say in the direction of the community. A database of verified holders is also maintained to keep track of those who hold the NFTs and to provide special perks to these members.
