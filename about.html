About Us

Digitally painted NFTs on Cardano


My background as an artist spans over a decade of pencil realism art, through which I captured nature and it's tranquil moments in all its detail. I've now decided it's pencils down, and have taken a step into the world of digital art.

The artworks for 'Rust In Peace' are a tribute to the healing power of nature, inviting you to pause and reflect on the majesty of the world around us. Whether you find solace in your peaceful surroundings or are inspired to venture out and explore, true serenity comes from connecting with the natural world.

Rust In Peace carries apocalyptic undertones that prompt us to contemplate our relationship with the environment. While the depicted scenario is not our current reality, it presents us with an opportunity to reflect on such a future and perhaps look to seek a more harmonious existence with the world around us.


Zanti (Rust In Peace Artist).
