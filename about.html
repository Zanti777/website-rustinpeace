About Us

Digitally painted NFTs on Cardano

Rust In Peace is a digital art series that explores the theme of nature reclaiming the earth from technology. The art is digitally painted and available as NFTs on the Cardano blockchain. The series portrays a world where nature has taken over abandoned technology, symbolizing a return to balance between man and the natural world.


The art is vibrant and colorful, depicting lush greenery growing over the remains of technological structures. It is a thought-provoking and visually stunning collection that invites viewers to consider the relationship between humanity and the natural world.
