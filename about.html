About Us

Digitally painted NFTs on Cardano


My artistic journey spans over a decade of pencil realism, where I meticulously captured nature's tranquil moments in intricate detail. Now, having set my pencils aside, I've embraced the digital realm to create the "Rust In Peace" collection.

These artworks serve as a tribute to nature's healing power, inviting viewers to pause and reflect on the majesty of our world. Whether you find solace in peaceful surroundings or feel inspired to venture outward and explore, true serenity emerges from connecting with the natural world.

"Rust In Peace" carries subtle apocalyptic undertones that spark imagination and curiosity about the resilient spirit of the natural world. These scenes invite you to embark on a visual adventure, exploring forgotten pathways where nature reclaims its domain. They celebrate the thrill of discovery and remind us of the endless wonders waiting to be found when we step off the beaten path and immerse ourselves in the wild.
