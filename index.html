WELCOME TO RUST IN PEACE



Imagine a world where the cities have been reclaimed by the wilderness and abandoned technology lies scattered, rusting amidst the overgrowth. The digital art series of Rust In Peace captures the essence of this hauntingly beautiful world where nature has taken back what was rightfully hers.

Rust In Peace is an exploration of an apocalyptic world. Each piece portrays a scene where the remnants of human technology are slowly being transformed and as you explore these pieces, you will find intricate details of ecosystems within the lost items.
